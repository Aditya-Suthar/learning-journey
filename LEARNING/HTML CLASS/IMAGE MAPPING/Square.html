RECTANGLE

A rectangle is a four-sided geometrical shape in which opposite sides are equal and parallel. All interior angles are right angles (90 degrees).

Properties of Rectangle

  • Number of sides: 4
  • Opposite sides are equal
  • All angles are 90 degrees
  • Diagonals are equal

Formula

Area = Length × Breadth

Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Breadth)
